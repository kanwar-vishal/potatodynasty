HOW TO MAKE VEG FRIED RICE

    INGREDIENTS OF VEG FRIED RICE

  • 1 bowl Rice boiled
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 2-3 cloves Garlic, chopped
  • 1 Red chilli, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Carrots, chopped
  • 3-4 Baby corn, chopped
  • 4-5 tbsp Cabbage (diced)
  • 1/2 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 4-5 Green beans, chopped
  • to taste Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 tsp Soy sauce
  • 2-3 tbsp Wine (optional)
  • To garnish Spring onion greens

    MAIN COURSE

  1. In a pan add 1 Tbsp oil, garlic and chilli. Saute.
  2. Add carrots, baby corn, green beans and cabbage. Saute and add sesame oil.
  3. Add the rice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, soy sauce and wine. Cook for a minute.
  5. Serve hot garnished with some chopped spring onion greens.
